Front-End Developer

Friendly, professional, stable working environment where one can grow in their career

As a Frontend/JavaScript Engineer, you will help us build beautiful and quick interfaces that play a daily mission critical role for some of our biggest client companies. You will work closely with a small or mid team of frontend and backend developers and have a say in the design future and in the functional and visual design of what you build day-to-day.You will collaborate with strategy, design, and development teams to convert prototypes into websites and web-based applications using your mastery of the JavaScript environment.

Requirements:

  • Strong JavaScript/TypeScript development skills, understanding of new ES6 concepts, and experience in at least one of their most used frameworks such as React, Vue or Angular (both JS or 2+)
  • Knowledge of jQuery, Bootstrap and HTML5/CSS3 as well as experience working with REST APIs
  • Experience with GIT source control systems
  • 1+ years of professionally building production-grade, mid or large-scale web software
  • Experience working in an Agile environment
  • Have passion and experience for building responsive, elegant and engaging experiences with future friendly web technologies.
  • Building complex user interactions either from scratch or within existing frameworks
  • Practice disciplined software engineering (e.g. debugging, automated testing, code reviews, document, writing clean and pragmatic code)
  • Understanding of further reaching technical concepts (API design, modular code structures, functional vs object-oriented programming, design patterns.)

Pre-requests:

  • Happy and ambitious personality and character (non-negotiable!)
  • A natural curiosity for new scripting languages, frameworks, and technologies
  • Self-motivated and passionate about development
  • A team player who is friendly and open-minded but also a result-driven and committed person
  • Self-awareness and continuous improvement mindset
  • BS Degree in Computer Science, Information Systems or equivalent work experience/education combination
  • Fluent English (reading, writing and speaking)
  • You know something very good? Share it so the other colleagues could learn from you. Knowledge transfer is one of the things we invest most of our time.

Internship program

70% of the people who were interns at Sourcico later on became full-time employees at our company

At Sourcico the Interns are treated like full time employees and are expected to go above and beyond to meet the customers goals

If you are enrolled in University or just finishing your education, but also if you’re visiting some of the private Academies in the city, and you are excited about programming or testing – always asking “What if?”, at Sourcico your curiosity matters!We will give you your initial experience with our 4 months Internship program because here the opportunities are endless and the knowledge sharing is one of the most important postulates.

At Sourcico the Interns are treated like full time employees and are expected to go above and beyond to meet the customers goals. You will have a chance to work with different clients being shifted to different projects and end up like one of our colleagues. For the four months spent in fast paced environment, training is key. Our internship program is focused completely ondevelopment, besides the welcoming atmosphere we will help you grow professionally, but also privately because we are looking for people that would fit our company culture, not just people that knows how to code. 70% of the people who were interns at Sourcico later on became full-time employees at our company, and 100% of the interns became Developers in the community. 

So, this is pretty much as a guarantee that in the 4 months that you spend here you’ll get the needed knowledge and experience.

We offer internships with a possibility for a full-time employment in the following areas:

  • Front-End development: React, Angular, Vue.
  • Back-End development: Node.js / C++
  • QA
  • Mobile development: Android, iOS, Hybrid and Native application

Qualities that matter to us the most:

  • Passion and enthusiasm for software development, as well as understanding the full development life-cycle and best practices
  • Ability to work and collaborate in team, capable of individual “deep work” as well
  • Strong time-management skills and can meet deadlines consistently
  • Takes direction well, but also able to make decisions and take initiative
  • Up-to-date on the latest web technologies, trends and initiatives
  • Professional, dependable, solid work ethic, detail-oriented, dedication to quality, self-motivated

Locations

Israel, Tel Aviv

Our Israeli branch is located in Tel Aviv and is serving mainly the Israeli high-tech industry, it’s well established clients as well as it’s younger and agile startups. Our Management, sales and Administration is based in Tel Aviv.

Skopje, Macedonia

Our competence center in Macedonia enjoys a huge talent pool of the finest engineers (around 6,000 in Skopje), in a friendly and beautiful country that has a very rapidly growing economy (3rd in growth rate in Europe 2014) specially the IT industry, a vivid developers community, all within a short flight away and only one hour time zone difference from Israel.

Chișinău, Moldova

In the heart of Moldova in addition to its famously good wines there is a healthy growing community of engineering & science serving as one of the most important growth engines of the local economy. The local IT community is significantly supported by the government and the Moldavian talent pool proved to be a perfect location for our second competence center.
